Why Invest In Punjab

All Approvals in 45 Days with Deemed Approval

  • Maximum timeline for approvals capped at 45 working days.
  • 83% of approvals notified for 30 days or less.
  • If no decision is made within the timeline, the portal auto-generates a deemed approval that is legally valid.
  • Deemed Approval the next day.
  • System-driven scrutiny, escalation up to Deputy Commissioner & Administrative Secretary.

Punjab Right to Business Act

  • Now applicable to projects with up to 125 Cr investment in plant & machinery, covering about 85% of industrial proposals including industry, IT, hospitals, hotels, and education.

Revenue Feasibility Report CRO Certificate in 15 Days

  • First-time digital Revenue Feasibility Certificate launched by Punjab – CRO certificate within 15 working days.
  • Verifying land ownership and siting elements – a key pre-approval for environment and construction permits.
  • Eliminating manual delays and enabling faster clearance for investors.

Plot Sub-Division Policy – Unlocking Land Potential

  • New policy allows easy subdivision of industrial plots for families, co-developers, and joint ventures.
  • Enabling flexible land use for redevelopment or monetization, boosting brownfield investments.
  • Clear timelines, eligibility, and safeguards to ensure transparency.

Compliance Self Certification Online

  • Fire NOC now valid for 1/3/5 years basis risk classification.
  • Height limit for industrial buildings increased to 21m.
  • Occupiers can self-certify compliance online.
  • Fire drawings schemes made by Empaneled architects now accepted by the Department.
  • 53-point checklist eliminated.

Conversion Policy for High - Value Development

  • 10 land use categories for individual industrial plots developments permitted (like IT, hotel, hospital, institute, commercial, industrial park etc.)
  • Clear norms for plot size and road width defined; 80 ft for most and 100 ft for commercial/ industrial parks.

Flexibility in Building Plan Verification & Building Stability Certificate for factories

  • Self-certification of structural safety of factory buildings under the Factory Act, 1948.
  • Redundant inspections removed.
  • Digital records and professional validation.

Compliance Overhauling of all 52 Focal Points by November 2025

  • 200 Cr budget allocated.
  • Bridging key infrastructure gaps in power, water, roads, drainage, and digital access.
  • A new industrial park maintenance model in partnership with industry to be launched soon.
